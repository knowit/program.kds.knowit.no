Knowit Developer Summit

October 24 - 25

Praktisk info

Knowit Developer Summit(KDS) is Knowits annual developer conference. The 2014 conference venue is the Radison Blue Stockholm Waterfront hotel. The conference is held from friday october 24 to saturday october 25.

Those of you who have booked hotel rooms at the conference hotel must check the reservation here

A complete list of delegates is found here

If you have questions during the conference, please feel free to send them to kds@knowit.no or tto@knowit.no (we read mail all the time) or you can call 00 47 98 25 78 22.

The venue

The conference department is on the second floor. It has its own entrance, to the left of the main hotel entrance. Use the escalators to go DOWN two floors. Our rooms are called "24 + 25", "26" and "27" and they are located innermost part of the conference department. None of the rooms are large enough to gather all the participants in, but we must still be all together on two occasions: When the conference starts for a little kick-off and during the planning of open spaces. We will manage if we stand up and are patient :-)

Pre-program lunsj

Lunch is served outside the meeting rooms on the second floor. It standing buffet and a good opportunity to chat and mingle before we start the conference. Please note that lunch is served in the conference area, NOT in the hotel

Conference dinner

There will be a conference dinner on friday from 19:00. Dinner is served in "Bar 5" on the 5th floor. Dinner is included in the conference fee and we hope that all of you will attend - Food and drinks are ordered for all the attendees

Social media

If you use social media during the conference, please use #KDS2014. We know that a lot of you and many other Knowit employees use yammer - Using yammer will make sure that the rest of Knowit is aware of KDS.

Late registration!

There are a couple of seats available if you want to attend the conference. It might be possible to fix accommodation as well, but this needs to be handled separately. Please contact us if you register late and need a hotel room.
